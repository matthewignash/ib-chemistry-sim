Combustion Calorimetry — Spirit Lamp
Measure the enthalpy of combustion by burning a fuel under a container of water. Heat loss to surroundings is the major systematic error — equipment choices directly affect accuracy.
Graphical Extrapolation: Click two points on the cooling portion to extrapolate back to the time the flame was extinguished.

Variables

Calorimeter material (polystyrene, glass, metal)
DV:
Temperature change / calculated enthalpy

Volume of solution, concentration of acid/base, mass of solid
