Teacher Notes
Sources of systematic error in gravimetric analysis:
  • Incomplete decomposition/dehydration (mass too high)
  • Moisture absorbed from air during cooling (mass too high)
  • Spattering during heating (mass too low)
  • Loss during transfer (mass too low)
  • Precipitate not fully dried (mass too high for precip method)
